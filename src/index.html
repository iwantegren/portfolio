About Me

Philosophy & Mindset

I'm a creator who builds products end-to-end: from user needs and UX flows to backend architecture, data modeling, and deployment.

I aim for pragmatic, maintainable systems—shipping iteratively, measuring what matters, and tightening correctness, security, and performance as the product grows.

I like to speak in outcomes and trade-offs, not just code.

Ownership 🧭

I take ownership of the product as a whole, not just individual components.

Simplicity 🧱

Prefer the simplest solution that scales; avoid cleverness that costs future velocity.

Security 🔐

Treat data protection as a baseline: least privilege, safe defaults, and defensive design.

Feedback Loops 📈

Ship, observe, learn: instrumentation, user behavior signals, and continuous refinement.
